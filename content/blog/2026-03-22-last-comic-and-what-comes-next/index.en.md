While the final touches are being added to the cover, I want to remind you that this year we’ll be stepping away from actively working on comics. Back in winter, I thought we’d release a couple of mini-comics this year and then stop, but I’ve decided that the current one will be the last. It’s already turned out to be quite substantial by our standards - 14 pages plus a fairly complex cover. 

What we’ll do next remains an open question. It’s possible we’ll continue working on comics and shelve it for later - until we have the resources to fully illustrate the pages. I already have one story in mind that would work well in the comic format. Of course, it’s not the only idea - there are several others, but they haven’t fully taken shape yet.

As for the upcoming story, we’ll return to the world of dwarves. That’s actually where I first started working on my comics. It’s just a coincidence that we’re ending there as well.

While working on the script and storyboard (my part is now complete), I spent a lot of time thinking about the world as a whole. For me, it used to feel somewhat empty - just a few interesting characters, a handful of locations and not much else. Over the past few months, that world has grown significantly, gaining small stories and new characters.

Still, none of that really matters as long as these ideas exist only in my head - it’s time to bring them out, put them on paper, and try to shape them into a cohesive story or leave them as a collection of separate tales, which is also a perfectly valid option for me.
